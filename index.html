Barold is a 2D Platformer with Medieval and Fantasy storyline.

You are Barold, a knight that has to navigate through hard and tricky jumps, fight bosses and most of all, dodge projectiles. Visit your old pals and ask them for help in search of Gothmog, a demon you used to call your friend.

Barold includes translations for Spanish and Russian and has two difficulties. Normal mode is the default version for casual gamers and Hard mode includes more challenges, specifically made for "God Gamers."
